Our Rafting Trips

Rapid Rush Adventure

Perfect for thrill-seekers, this half-day trip takes you through the most intense rapids on the river. Tackle Class III and IV rapids with experienced guides who ensure your safety as you plunge through waves, drops, and fast-moving water. This adventure guarantees an adrenaline-packed experience for those looking to challenge themselves.

Full-Day Rapids Challenge

Ready for a full day of adventure? This trip covers a long stretch of the river with both challenging rapids and relaxing sections for a balanced experience. Enjoy a riverside lunch and moments to swim in quieter spots. The Full-Day Rapids Challenge is ideal for intermediate to experienced rafters looking for an all-encompassing river journey.

Wilderness Expedition

For the ultimate adventure, embark on a multi-day rafting trip through untouched wilderness. Spend nights camping along the river, with meals provided and stunning scenery all around. Encounter challenging rapids, explore hidden coves, and immerse yourself in nature. This trip is designed for true outdoor enthusiasts and offers an unforgettable experience in the wild.
